Affordable Education Assistance

We know how expensive it can be to pursue a higher education. For college students who have limited time to work outside of their studies, we offer reduced prices so that academic tutoring can fit within your budget. For high school students, we offer reduced prices based on the tutoring time frame you sign-up for (e.g., signing up for a month of tutoring qualifies students for lower prices). See the table below for an estimate of how much tutoring may cost.

Appointment Length High School College
2 Weeks 45min-1.5hrs $25/hr $22/hr
3 Weeks 45min-1.5hrs $23/hr $20/hr
1 Month 45min-1.5hrs $21/hr $18/hr
2 Months 45min-1.5hrs $20/hr $17/hr

Note: Prices are not guaranteed and may fluctuate. Contact us to secure your exact hourly rate.
