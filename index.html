"Two roads diverged in a wood, and I, I took the one less traveled by, And that has made all the difference." -Robert Frost

My collegiate career began at a community college, which seemed atypical compared to my peers. Nevertheless, I believe I learned a valuable lesson in taking the road less traveled by. During my first year at my community college, I was accepted into a guaranteed admission transfer program to the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Having experienced both a large university and a small community college, I am accustomed to various class structures and difficulties. In the span of four years, I earned two degrees and graduated debt free by taking the road less traveled by. I strive to apply this same mindset in how I support students in reaching their academic goals by considering all possible avenues.
