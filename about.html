About Us

We offer online, 1-on-1 tutoring that caters to your schedule and unique academic needs. Courses range from high school through college for personalized mentorship. Contact us today to get started.

Business Inquiries and General Questions

SWIFT ONLINE TUTORING IS AN ONLINE TUTORING SERVICE BASED IN THE TRIANGLE AREA.

We are a remote tutoring service based in the Chapel Hill area. We offer high school as well as collegiate tutoring. Other grade levels can be tutored on a case-by-case basis. We tutor STEM subjects, teach collegiate study skills, help format collegiate research papers, and more. For more information on our services, see the "Academic Levels" section in the navigation bar.

Learning Beyond the Course Material

We help students develop the necessary study skills for high school and beyond by guiding them through how to structure their course material. Our job is not to re-teach material, but to foster a learning environment where students can ask questions and receive more immediate feedback. Swift Online Tutoring strives to emulate how to be an informed student that asks questions and can evaluate a problem on their own. We work hard to adhere to your individual tutoring needs in a way that models how to succeed in a high school classroom or a college lecture hall.

Collegiate Resources

We also offer college preparation resources for high school students

For current college students, we can help format research papers in accordance with the most recent APA/MLA standards. Likewise, we offer helpful online studying materials as well as informative resources for course registration. For soon-to-be college students (i.e., high school juniors and seniors), we can help you find prospective majors and figure out where to start your collegiate career. Each student's collegiate path is vastly different and we have various online resources and programs for local students planning on remaining in-state.

Aren't sure where to begin your college search? Reach out and we are more than happy to assist!
